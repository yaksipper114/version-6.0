Lesson 1: Dot Plots

Description: â€‹This video introduces concepts like mean, median, range, and mode, through a dot plot. This is our first video for Algebra I and introduces one-variable statistics.

Vocab:
Mean: Average number given a data set.
Mode: Most common occuring number.
Median: The number in the middle of a data set, when the data set is in numerical order.
Range: Maximum minus the minimum numbers in a data set.
Maximum: Highest number in a data set.
Minimum: Lowest number in a data set.
